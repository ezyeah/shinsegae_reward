ctitle-wrap
  • 기본 구조

    .ctitle-wrap-{$gap} > left-area > stitle / ctitle / stext | right-area

  • 하단 여백에 따라 class 사용
    • ctitle-wrap-xl margin-bottom: 30px
    • ctitle-wrap-lg margin-bottom: 24px
    • ctitle-wrap-md margin-bottom: 16px
    • ctitle-wrap-sm margin-bottom: 10px
    • ctitle-wrap-xs margin-bottom: 6px
